Description

Eat, shoot, dash, and explode your way through the cosmos in this adrenaline-fuelled arcade action game. WORMHOLE is a roguelike twist on the classic snake format featuring a stackable perk system and 100+ hand-crafted levels.

History

Features

  • Chomp your way through 100+ handcrafted levels in the main mode, and see how long you can survive your favourite stages in marathon mode
  • Become extinction incarnate with three abilities (DASH, CLAW, and YEEHAW), each offering a unique flavour of destruction
  • Maximize your destructive potential using a variety of stackable perks designed to create cosmic chaos
  • Experience the dark, vast expanses of space in a black and white style inspired by early arcade games. If spooky minimalism's not your speed, choose from over 60 delicious unlockable colour palettes to flavour the galaxy any way you like.
  • Enjoy 40+ original tracks, ranging from spooky space goth to high energy gabber bangers.
